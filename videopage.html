Case Study: Active Sussex This Girl Can Campaign

Active Sussex are the County Sports Partnership of Sussex, tasked with increasing participation in sport and physical activity. They represent and manage Sport England's national This Girl Can campaign locally.

Active Sussex had recently recruited an Ambassador in Sussex to promote the campaign and wanted to produce inspiring video content showing the Ambassador trying new sports, with the aim of the campaign to encourage women of all shapes and sizes to try something new. This was a first time for presenter Tess so she was a little nervous but we had lots of fun filming this project.

The case study below shows a short upbeat film that tells the story of Tess trying skateboarding with the UK number 1 female skateboarder Lucy Adams and Brighton group She Shredders. I used a main camera and a body camera on the presenter to really get a feel for the excitement of this challenging activity.

"Videos can be very time consuming to produce, but Laura took the pain out of the process. She was professional from the start to the finish of the process. She accurately interpreted our brief and the look and feel that we were going for - we barely had any amends to feed back. Laura used her creativity and chose great music, to make the video young, fun and full of life. Once the video was ready to be published, she even offered to upload the video to our YouTube account and suggested key words to boost our SEO ranking. I would definitely recommend working with Laura."

- Joanna Savage, PR & Comms, Active Sussex
